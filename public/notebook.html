Notebook flow

1) Data foundation

Yahoo Finance download, quality checks, log-return transformation, and train/validation/test split (70/15/15).

2) Diagnostics first

Stationarity and residual diagnostics (ADF, KPSS, Ljung-Box, ARCH-LM) before fitting production-style models.

3) Modeling stack

ARIMA for conditional mean + GARCH family (GARCH/GJR/FIGARCH/EGARCH) for volatility, selected with validation AIC logic.

4) Evaluation

Rolling backtests (60-day + 365-day robustness), horizon analysis, DM tests against naive/EWMA, and VaR backtests via Kupiec/Christoffersen.
